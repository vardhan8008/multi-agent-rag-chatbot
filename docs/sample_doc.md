# Northwind Co. — Customer Handbook

Northwind Co. is a fictional company used for testing this RAG assistant.

## About

Northwind builds collaborative note-taking software for small teams. The
product launched in 2021 and is used by more than 5,000 teams worldwide.

## Plans and pricing

We offer three plans:

- **Free** — for individuals. 1 user, 100 notes, community support. $0.
- **Pro** — for growing teams. Unlimited notes, version history, priority
  support. $20 per user per month.
- **Enterprise** — for large organisations. SSO, audit logs, dedicated success
  manager. Custom pricing, contact sales.

## Return and refund policy

Northwind accepts returns within 30 days of delivery for a full refund, as long
as the item is unused and in its original packaging. Refunds are processed
within 5–7 business days to the original payment method.

## Support

You can reach support at support@northwind.example or through the in-app chat,
Monday to Friday, 9am–6pm CT. For urgent issues, call +1-555-0100.

## Data and security

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Enterprise customers can enable
single sign-on (SSO) and export an audit log of workspace activity. Northwind
is SOC 2 Type II compliant and performs annual penetration testing.
